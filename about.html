About Us

Our main priority is ensuring the health and well-being of all our animals.

Register Today

We are a small in home TICA registered cattery, located in Wingham, Ontario. To ensure we provide your family with only the best Bengal kittens, all of our cats are health tested and screened as negative / non carriers for over 50 predispositions including Progressive Retinal Atrophy, Pyruvate Kinase (PK), Feline Leukemia (FeLV) and feline immunodeficiency virus (FIV). In addition to coming from HCM clear lines. Our cats are fully vaccinated and on parasite prevention all year round.
